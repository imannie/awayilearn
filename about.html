About me

Annie Phuong Tran, born in Vietnam and currently based in San Francisco, is the founder and editor behind Annie x Things.


Born in Saigon, Vietnam, and currently based in San Francisco. I am a brand new Grad Student with major is International Business on May 2018 and have been working in the Education field since 2015, but my love of web/apps design dates back to 2016. After years of systems administrion, I decided it was time to pursue a new challenge, and a career change to front end development was made. In September I became a Fall cohort at Kickstart Coding, and is on schedule to complete my studied. I now spends my days writing HTML, CSS and Bootstrap with future to learn Full Stack completely

The blog started as my very first project on my way learn the sweetbitter non-human languages and is an online collection of articles about my love for technology, travel, fashion, entrepreneur and life as well. Having studied International Business at Lincoln University, I seek to build a reading community that is equally excited about my passions.

The blog marries my pursuit of creating quality reading content with my love for photography. And it is my hope that the resulting images and articles on this blog capture world perfectly - affectionate, familial, and above all.
